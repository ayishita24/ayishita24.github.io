THEME OF THE YEAR

"Lucem" is a Latin word that carries the meaning of "light" in various contexts.

Light symbolises illumination, knowledge and understanding. Metaphorically, the term can be used to convey the idea of clarity, insight and enlightenment.

It's often employed to represent the dispelling of darkness or ignorance, highlighting the concept of gaining awareness and seeing things from a new perspective.

The term "Lucem" captures the powerful symbolism of light as a source of guidance and revelation.

ABOUT US

The purpose of Rotaract is to provide an opportunity for young men and woman to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and to promote better relationships between all people worldwide, through a framework of friendship and service.

Our Background

We are an integral part of the Rotaract which is a worldwide organization of young men and women aged 15 to 30 based in over 176 countries. The Rotaract Club of FR.CRCE comes under Zone 1A and district 3141.

What we do ?

  • We are a non-profit organisation committed to unlocking the highest potential of our members and have been leading change in various spheres.
  • We identify, process, and understand the problems that directly impact our lives and communities.
  • We believe that the youth is uniquely positioned to build more than just an inclusive world.
